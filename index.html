What this tool does
1
You run a bookmarklet on your Sokoview admin to collect all portfolio data
2
Claude generates a personalised monthly performance email for each subscriber
3
Preview each email, test with your own address, then send to all subscribers
Step 1 — Install the bookmarklet
Drag the button below to your browser bookmarks bar. You only need to do this once.
📊 Sokoview — Collect Portfolios
← Drag this to your bookmarks bar
Step 2 — Run it on your admin panel
  1. Open sokoview.co.tz/admin/portfolios in your browser (make sure you're logged in)
  2. Click the 📊 Sokoview — Collect Portfolios bookmark you just added
  3. A panel will appear — click "Collect all portfolios" and wait for it to finish
  4. Click "Download JSON" — this saves sokoview-portfolios.json
Step 3 — Upload the JSON file
